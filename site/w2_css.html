CLOUD 9 BAKERY

Who we are

Welcome to our website! Cloud 9 Bakery is a Singapore-based bread bakery originally from Germany that creates world-class bread in the little red dot. We focus on making high quality, warm-on-delivery Roggenmischbrot, Vollkornbrot, and Weizenkornbroetchen for our customers. The bread is delivered on Saturday and Sunday mornings at 6 am, fresh to your doorstep.

How it works

You've already found the right website. Simply add the bread that you want, choose one-time order or subscription, and check out. You can pay easily with multiple payment options. The bread will be delivered to you at your desired time, warm, crispy, and crunchy!

All our Cloud 9 Bakery yeast is imported freshly from Siegburg, Northrhine-Westfalia, Germany.
